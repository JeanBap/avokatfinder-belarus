Razresheniye na Stroitelstvo (Дазвол на будаўніцтва)

The building permit required for lawful construction in Belarus

Razresheniye na stroitelstvo is the administrative permit authorising construction of a new building or major reconstruction of an existing one in Belarus. It is issued by the local executive committee (mestniy ispolnitelny komitet) or its authorised body and is required before any significant construction work commences.

To obtain a building permit, the applicant must submit approved architectural plans prepared by a licensed design organisation, a site location document confirming the land's intended use, environmental assessments where required, and the relevant application form with payment of state fees.

How It Works in Practice

Belarus has faced a significant problem of unauthorised construction, particularly private residential buildings (dachas and country houses) built without permits. While some legalisation procedures exist, not all structures can be regularised, and buyers of illegally built properties face demolition orders and fines.

Upon completion of construction, the builder must obtain a completion certificate (akt priemki v ekspluatatsiyu) confirming the building was constructed in accordance with the permit and relevant technical regulations. The completion certificate is required before the building can be registered in the unified state register.

Key Facts

New Build CheckVerify both permit and completion certificate

Buyers of newly constructed or recently renovated properties should verify both the building permit and the completion certificate. Properties sold without a completion certificate cannot be registered in the buyer's name and cannot be legally occupied.

Common Mistake: Buyers of newly built private houses sometimes discover that the seller began construction before the permit was issued, or that the completed building differs from the approved plans. Either situation blocks registration and may require costly rectification.
Expert Tip: For new builds, request certified copies of the building permit and the approved plans, then compare the approved plans to the actual building before signing. Any deviations should be regularised by the seller before the purchase completes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the razresheniye na stroitelstvo?

It is the building permit issued by the local executive committee authorising construction or major reconstruction. Without it, construction is illegal.

What is the akt priemki v ekspluatatsiyu?

It is the completion certificate issued after construction, confirming the building complies with the permit and technical standards. It is required for registration and legal occupation.

Can illegal buildings be legalised in Belarus?

Some legalisation procedures exist, but not all structures can be regularised. Buyers of potentially illegal structures should investigate legalisation eligibility before purchasing.
